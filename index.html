Our Vision

We aspire to lead globally, offering accessible luxury across borders.

  • Dedicated to innovation and quality excellence.
  • Committed to creating meaningful experiences for our customers.
  • Striving for sustainability and ethical practices worldwide.


Our Mission

Achieving customer delight through business innovation and cost-effectiveness while pursuing the latest industry trends for creating stakeholder values by rapidly growing our turnover and profitability.

  • Focused on delivering exceptional customer experiences.
  • Embracing innovation and cost-efficient practices.
  • Driving growth to enhance stakeholder value.


Our Purpose

Silvan Group: To provide floor tiles to 5,000 families who are in poverty and unable to afford them by 2030.

  • Committed to supporting underprivileged communities.
  • Striving for impactful social responsibility initiatives.
  • Dedicated to achieving this goal by 2030.


Our Values

Our core values shape every aspect of our organization and guide us in our journey to excellence.

  • Responsibility: Upholding accountability in all our actions.
  • Customer Service: Ensuring exceptional care and satisfaction.
  • Quality: Delivering excellence in every product and service.
  • Hospitality: Fostering warmth and a welcoming spirit.
  • Inclusivity: Embracing diversity and promoting equality.
